CANAPE
A canapé is a type of hors d'oeuvre, a small, prepared, and often decorative food, consisting of a small piece of bread wrapped or topped with some savoury food, held in the fingers and often eaten in one bite. The composition of a canapé consists of a base (e.g., the bread or pancake), a spread, a main item, and a garnish.
