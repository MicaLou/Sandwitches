RECIPE:


BITE-SIZED SANDWICH CANAPE

Ingredients                                                              Nutritive Value

  1. Bread (White)
  2. Ham
  3. Cheese (cheddar)
  4. Mayonnaise
  5. Tomato
  6. Lettuce
    7.
                                                                                                  

STEPS

  1. Prepare everything you need. Including the ingredients,
    the tools, the utensils, and the equipment listed.

  2. Wash the tomatoes and lettuce

  3. Cut the Tomatoes

  4. Now, toast the bread.

  5. Once the bread has finished toasting, cut them into 4 smaller square pieces.

  6. Put Mayonnaise on the bread and spread evenly. Put them aside

  7. Now, You can start frying the meat. Fry your ham.

  8. The next step is to cut the ham into the same shape as the bread.

  9. Now, you can start arranging the ingredients into a sandwich. Add a toothpick, and then put it on the plate.

GARNISH

  1. For the garnish, just cut small pieces of tomatoes and lettuce and plate them. Now you can serve!

FINAL OUTPUT:
