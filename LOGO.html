The Logo

                         

For our business, we have made this logo to highlight the main topic or main product we wish to show on our website which is Sandwiches. The business name is Sand-witches. We chose this business name because we wish to highlight the sandwiches, we made during our cookery class. Not only those recipes, but other recipes as well. As you can see the “sandwich” part, the “witch” part signifies that our sandwiches seem to be magical and enthralling, like a witch’s work.

The Witch Hat in the logo represents the fascinating and magical works of a witch towards our recipes in sandwiches. It shows that we serve a different kind of sandwich with a little magic and deliciousness in our sandwiches.

The sandwich represents the main product of the business. Sandwiches. They are unique sandwiches in many ways. It could be the shape, the ingredients, or the taste. But anyhow they are magical and satisfying to eat. These sandwiches are mostly from our cookery class where we were tasked to add a special ingredient to differ from the original recipe.

Lastly, the Spatula and the Broomstick signify the magical cookery of sandwiches in our kitchen. I and my partner are behind these fantastical sandwiches on our website. We present “Sand-Witches”.
