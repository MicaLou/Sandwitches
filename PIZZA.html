RECIPE:


BITE-SIZED PIZZA CANAPE

Ingredients                                                              Nutritive Value

  1. Bread (toasted)
  2. Pepperoni
  3. Cheese
  4. Tomato Sauce
  5. Butter or Margarine
  6. Salt
  7. Tomato Slices
  8. mushrooms
                                                                                                  

STEPS

  1. Prepare everything you need. Including the ingredients,
    the tools, the utensils, and the equipment listed.

  2. Cut your bread into bite-sized circular shapes

  3. Spread butter on the circular shaped bread and
    spread evenly.
    Put on baking pan after

  4. Put tomato sauce as the base of your Canape.

  5. Put the pepperoni after you've spread the tomato sauce.

  6. After putting the pepperoni, pinch off pieces of mushrooms and put it on the Canape

  7. Now, your canape is ready to toast. Put your Canape inside the oven and set the
    temperature at 180oC. Toast for 5-6 mins.

  8. When it has finished toasting, Take out the tray from the oven and let it cool.
    while cooling, prepare the garnish

GARNISH

  1. Wash a tomato and cut it into 4 pieces. Throw out the insides and leave the skin

  2. Place the tomato slices into place (view picture) and then place the Canapes on the plate. Garnish it with tomato Sauce as well.
    And finally serve!
