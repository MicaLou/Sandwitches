RECIPE:

   
ClubHouse Sandwich
A club sandwich, also called a clubhouse sandwich, is a sandwich consisting of bread, sliced cooked poultry, fried bacon, lettuce, tomato, and mayonnaise. It is often cut into quarters or halves and held together by cocktail sticks.

Ingredients                                                              Nutritive Value

  1. Loaf Bread
  2. Bacon
  3. Ham
  4. Boiled Eggs
  5. Lettuce
  6. Tomatoes
  7. Sliced Cheese
  8. Ranch Dressing
  9. Potatoes
  10. Mayonnaise
  11. Pickled Relish
  12. Butter
  13. salt
  14. Water
  15. oil
                                                                                                  

STEPS

  1. Prepare everything you need. Including the ingredients,
    the tools, the utensils, and the equipment listed.

  2. Cut the bread into the desired shape.
    Use a bread cutter with a circular rim if needed

  3. Wash and Cut the fresh ingredients and set them aside

  4. Start preparing the filling. One person,
    start preparing boiled eggs. Take a pot, fill it with 1-2 cups of water,
    Mix in a teaspoon of salt and boil 3 eggs for 7-8 mins on medium heat.

  5. As one person prepares the boiled eggs, another
    person can start cooking the meat.
    Take a frying pan and put cooking oil. Heat up the oil.
    Once it is heated up, take caution in putting the meat on the pan.
    Cook the ham till it is lightly cooked and the bacon till golden brown.

  6. While 2 people focus on the eggs and meat,
    another can prepare our garnish.
    The garnish is potato chips. Wash the potatoes.
    Once done, cut them into thin slices.
    Take a cooking pot and put oil enough for deep-frying.
    Deep fry the potatoes until golden brown and set aside.

  7. At this point, the fillings that need to be cooked are cooked.
    Now, you will take your
    shaped bread and toast them.
    Spread butter on your pan and toast in medium heat until
    golden brown

  8. Once weâ€™ve finished toasting the bread,
    we can finally arrange the sandwich. Spread ranch dressing on the
    first piece of bread (Dressing side-up) Then put the lettuce,
    cheese slice, ham, egg, and bacon (In order). Then Top
    with the second slice of bread. On top of the second slice, place
    lettuce tomato slices, ham, egg, and bacon. Spread ranch sauce
    on the third piece of bread (Dressing side-down) and place it on top of the fillings.
    Place the sandwich on the serving plate and place toothpicks.

GARNISH

  1. Arrange the fried potato chips on the plate beside the sandwich. Grab a mixing bowl and mix mayonnaise and pickled relish. Mix well so that the flavors combine. Put the sauce in a smaller bowl/saucer and place it on the serving plate. Sprinkle salt and pepper on the potato chips

  2. Grab a small piece of lettuce and shape it into a bow. Place it on top of the sandwich. Slice a cherry tomato in half and place it in the middle of the lettuce.
